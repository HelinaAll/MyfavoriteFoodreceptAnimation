Crispy chicken wrap

Ingredients Quanitity
Wheat tortillas 1 pack
Chrispy breading for chicken 1 pack
Chicken fillet 500g
Sour cream 300ml
Iceberg lettuce At your own request
Grated parmesan cheese 45ml
Oil 2spl
Green jalapeno sauce At your own request
Chili mayonnaise At your own request

Preparation

  • 1. Cut the chicken fillets into strips and bake in the breading according to the instructions on the package.
  • 2. Cut the salad into strips and mix in the sour cream.
  • 3. Heat the tortillas and spread the salad and chicken pieces on them. Top with grated parmesan, jalapeno sauce and cili mayonnaise.
  • 4. Fold and enjoy!
